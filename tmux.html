tmux fans assemble!

Posted on Thu, Jul 11, 2019 UNIX tmux

Most developers are used to some terminal multiplexer tool while working on shared environments (SSHing to a remote machine) or even managing multiple operations effectively. tmux, screen are such useful tools in the domain. The gains are

  • Organizing multiple terminal sessions without getting lost in a plethora of tabs (through appropriate naming and sub-grouping)
  • Preserving history of commands and outputs (enabling ability to resume from the terminal position exactly where left off)
  • Asynchronously executing command-line programs (the usefulness lies in running long-running programs without dependency on the driver / client)

This short article / cheatsheet aggregates some of the most useful tmux commands to help you make most of it. While learning CLI tools like tmux or vim has some initial steep curve, productivity is a definite outcome.
